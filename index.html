About the Author

Jack Baker was born in Hampshire, UK, and now lives in the United States. As a child he discovered his love of books, reading, and stories, devouring the works of C.S. Lewis, J.R.R. Tolkien, James Branch Cabell, Edgar Allan Poe. Jack is credited with being one of the creators of modern comics, as well as an author whose work crosses genres and reaches audiences of all ages.
